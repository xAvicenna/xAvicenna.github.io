Mechanical Keyboards!

This page is still under construction.

I type on a KBC Poker ii with cherry MX blue switches. I ordered a set of Pulse SA keycaps from massdrop in early September and they are still yet to arrive (but I'm patiently waiting). The board itself is a little high up, so I made a wrist rest in my carpentry class. It turned out really well. I used padouk which is an African species and smells amazing when cut (a mix between rootbeer and vanilla: delicious!) I enjoy typing even through I'm pretty slow (~60 WPM) and don't practice. As out of character as it might be for me, I can't wait for Enlish next semester so I can type a bunch of essays.

The previous image is the same keyboard I have with a similar keycap set to the one I had ordered. That specific keyboard is Reddit user Harbec. /r/mechanicalkeybaords is a very nice community containing a bunch of fanatics who spend too much on keyboards. But who am I to judge - I'm one of them (:
